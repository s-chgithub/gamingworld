Ubisoft Entertainment SA formerly Ubi Soft Entertainment SA is a French video game company headquartered in Saint-Mandé
with development studios across the world. Its video game franchises include
Assassin's Creed, Far Cry, For Honor, Just Dance, Prince of Persia, Rabbids, Rayman, Tom Clancy's, and Watch Dogs.
